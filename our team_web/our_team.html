Education Center Director


The Education Center Director is responsible for overseeing all operations of the Education Center. This includes leading and mentoring staff, developing educational programs, managing budgeting and fundraising activities, ensuring compliance with applicable regulations and standards, as well as collaborating with other education institutions. The Director will also ensure customer service excellence by administering surveys to evaluate our services, meeting departmental goals set by upper management, and addressing any student grievances.
